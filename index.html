History of willow trees

  • Ancient chinese people believed that willow branches would ward off evil spirits.
  • And it is the said the first ever willow tree grew in Babylon where Israelian children were kept for slavery.
  • Willow trees also go along with alot of Folklore and Mythology because the willow tree symbolises flexibility and adaptment.

    • Fun Facts

  • Rare willows can grow to the size of 70 feet.
  • They are one of the most fastest growing plants in the world.
  • If you plant a willow branch in the ground a new tree will grow in its place.
  • Willow trees grow near water filled areas like lakes and swamps.
